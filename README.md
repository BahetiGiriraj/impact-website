# IMPACT 🌟  
## Office of Student Affairs  
### MIT Art, Design and Technology University

IMPACT is the official student leadership and engagement platform of MIT ADT University, designed to showcase student communities, flagship events, cultural celebrations, leadership initiatives, and campus experiences.

The website acts as a digital hub connecting students, clubs, councils, and the university community through innovation, creativity, and collaboration.

IMPACT represents the spirit of student leadership, cultural diversity, innovation, and community engagement at MIT ADT University.

Built for the Office of Student Affairs, the platform highlights:

The platform creates a centralized experience for students to stay informed, connected, and involved.

# 🎉 Flagship Events

## 🎭 Spectra

SPECTRA is MIT ADT University's flagship cultural extravaganza showcasing dance, music, drama, and artistic performances.

The event celebrates:
- Classical dance
- Contemporary performances
- Music competitions
- Interactive workshops
- Cultural creativity

Multiple successful editions including:
- SPECTRA 5.0
- SPECTRA 6.0
- SPECTRA 8.0
- SPECTRA 9.0
- SPECTRA 11.0

have established Spectra as one of the university’s most celebrated cultural experiences.

---

## 💃 Garba Festival

MIT ADT Dandiya Nights is the university’s grand Navaratri celebration hosted at the MIT Samarth Ramdas Swami Sports Complex.

### Event Highlights
- 13,000+ participants
- Traditional Garba & Dandiya
- Gujarati cultural performances
- DJ fusion nights
- Festive lighting & décor
- Community bonding activities

The celebration blends tradition and modern entertainment while promoting unity, culture, and collective celebration.

---

## 🪔 Ganesh Chaturthi

The Ganesh Festival at MIT ADT University celebrates the spirit of devotion, creativity, and togetherness.

The festival includes:
- Beautiful clay idol installations
- Traditional rituals
- Cultural performances
- Student participation
- Decorative campus celebrations

The event strengthens the spirit of unity and cultural harmony across the campus community.

---

## 🚀 Persona Fest

Persona is MIT ADT University’s flagship techno-cultural fest and one of the largest student-driven celebrations on campus.

### Key Features
- 3-Day Mega Event
- Technical Competitions
- Cultural Performances
- Leadership Activities
- Creative Showcases
- Innovation Platforms

Persona promotes leadership, creativity, innovation, teamwork, and holistic student development.
